London

London, the capital of England and the United Kingdom, is a 21st-century city with history stretching back to Roman times.


At its centre stand the imposing Houses of Parliament, the iconic ‘Big Ben’ clock tower and Westminster Abbey, site of British monarch coronations. Across the Thames River, the London Eye and the entire city.
